Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 April 2026

This policy explains what information Explore Invergordon collects when you use this website, how we use it, and what rights you have. We keep things simple — this is a small, local website and we collect as little data as possible.

1. Who We Are

Explore Invergordon is a local tourism guide for Invergordon, Scottish Highlands, Scotland. The website is operated by Steve Redford, based in Invergordon, Highland, Scotland.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at: hello@exploreinvergordon.scot

2. What Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information. Here is what may be collected depending on how you use the site:

Contact form
If you use the contact form on our website, you will be asked to provide your name and email address, along with your message. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry and is not shared with third parties or used for marketing purposes.

Automatically collected information
Like most websites, certain basic technical information may be collected automatically when you visit, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This data is collected by the third-party services described below and is not stored directly by us.

3. Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy and data practices:

Google Maps
We use the Google Maps API to display an interactive map. Google may collect data about your use of the map, including your IP address and location data if you permit it. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Fonts
We use Google Fonts, loaded from Google's servers. When you visit the site, your browser makes a request to Google's servers to load these fonts, which may include your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Search Console
We use Google Search Console to understand how our site performs in search results. This collects aggregate, anonymised data about site visitors. We do not have access to personally identifiable information through this service.

Cloudflare Pages
Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect basic traffic data as part of its hosting and security services. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

Explore Invergordon does not set any first-party cookies of its own. However, third-party services we use (Google Maps, Google Fonts) may set cookies in your browser. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking, or profiling.

5. How We Use Your Information

Any personal information you provide (such as through the contact form) is used only to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or message
  • Improve the website and its content

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not send unsolicited emails or newsletters.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

Contact form submissions are retained only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry, and are then deleted. We do not maintain long-term databases of personal information.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

As a UK-based website, we comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR). You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing — you can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to object — you can object to us processing your data
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@exploreinvergordon.scot. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. External Links

Our website contains links to external websites (such as local businesses, tourist attractions, and partner organisations). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and recommend you review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
