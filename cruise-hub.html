Questions

Frequently Asked

How quickly will my itinerary arrive? +
We aim to send all itineraries within 24 hours of payment. We recommend ordering at least 24–48 hours before your port date for the best experience.
What format does it come in? +
You'll receive a clear, well-formatted email with your full itinerary — easy to screenshot or save to your phone. No app needed, no login required.
Is this genuinely personalised or a template? +
It's genuinely personalised. We read your answers carefully and build your plan around your specific interests, group, port times, and pace. We don't send a pre-written template.
Who writes the itinerary? +
Explore Invergordon is run locally from the Scottish Highlands. Your itinerary is built on real local knowledge — the kind of advice you'd get from a knowledgeable friend, not a tourist board leaflet.
How does the £15 payment work? +
After filling in your details, you'll be taken straight to a secure card payment form on the same page — powered by SumUp. Enter your card details, pay £15, and you're done. No redirect, no separate link. Your itinerary is written and sent once payment is confirmed.
What if I need to change my details after submitting? +
Just reply to our email before payment and we'll update everything. Once the itinerary has been written and sent, changes may not be possible — so do let us know as soon as you can.
Can I get a refund? +
If we haven't started your itinerary yet, we'll refund in full. Once it's been written and sent, refunds aren't possible — but we'll always do our best to make sure you're happy with what you receive.
