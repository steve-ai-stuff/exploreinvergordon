Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a Shore Day itinerary?

We typically design half-day (4–5 hours) itineraries that fit most cruise schedules. Full-day options available on request.

Can the itinerary be customised for my group?

Absolutely. That's exactly the point. We tailor every itinerary to your group's specific interests, fitness level, and preferences. No two itineraries are the same.

How do I book activities or restaurants?

Your itinerary includes phone numbers, opening hours, and booking instructions for all recommended venues. We'll let you know which ones need pre-booking and which are walk-in.

Is there a guide or group discount?

We provide a detailed itinerary. You can arrange a private local guide separately if preferred (we can recommend partners). Group discounts at some attractions are noted in your itinerary.

What if our ship is delayed?

Let us know. We can adjust the itinerary timing or provide a revised version if your visit date shifts.

Can you help with post-cruise planning?

Yes. If you're staying in the Highlands longer, we can extend the itinerary to include the NC500, Skye, and other region highlights.
